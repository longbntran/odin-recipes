Weeknight Wellington

Description

This beef Wellington recipe is perfect for entertaining if you want to impress your guests. It was given to me when I lived in England. It's best served with the center slightly pink.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ pounds beef tenderloin
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened, divided
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • ½ cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 2 ounces liver pate
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • (17.5 ounce) package frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 large egg yolk, beaten
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons red wine

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Place beef tenderloin in a small baking dish. Spread 2 tablespoons softened butter over beef.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until browned, 10 to 15 minutes
  4. Increase oven temperature to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  5. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat
  6. Mix together paté and remaining 2 tablespoons softened butter in a bowl
  7. Roll out puff pastry dough to about 1/4-inch thickness and place beef in the center.
  8. Fold up and seal all the edges, making sure the seams are not too thick
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes
  10. place reserved pan juices in a small saucepan over high heat
  11. Serve hot and enjoy!
